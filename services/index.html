Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my tune appointment?

Bring the bike in mechanical good health, at least half a tank of fresh premium fuel, and a list of current mods (exhaust, intake, cams, displacement).

How long does a full dyno tune usually take?

Most sessions are 2–3 hours with DynoAI, depending on setup complexity and baseline condition. You leave with before/after dyno sheets.

What changes the final price?

Complex hardware combinations, troubleshooting time, and custom build requirements can shift pricing. We confirm scope before the session starts.

Do you handle group or event tuning?

Yes. Group/event service is available across Central New York for 5+ bikes. Submit a request and we will coordinate logistics and schedule.
