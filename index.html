How It Works
01

One-Pull Baseline™

A single wide-open-throttle pull captures AFR data across the entire RPM range. DynoAI builds a physics model of your engine's breathing pattern from this data alone.

02

AI-Generated VE Table

Instead of correcting only the cells you tested, DynoAI uses engine physics to predict and fill the entire VE table — including part-throttle and cruise cells most shops never touch.

03

Real-Time Safety

During every pull, DynoAI monitors knock, AFR deviation, and torque curves in real time. If something looks wrong, the pull is aborted before your engine gets hurt.

04

Refine & Validate

2-4 refinement pulls dial in the calibration. You get full dyno sheets, before/after numbers, and a complete explanation of every change — no black box, no mystery.

Who DynoAI Is For
Stop Guessing. Start Modeling.

Traditional tuning is a repetitive loop: make a pull, read the AFR, adjust a few VE cells, flash the ECU, repeat. You might do this 15-20 times before the map is dialed in — and you're still only correcting the cells you actually tested.

DynoAI takes a fundamentally different approach. It uses physics-based volumetric efficiency modeling to understand how your engine breathes, then generates a complete VE table from as few as one WOT pull.

The math is simple: Correction Factor = Target AFR / Measured AFR. DynoAI applies this principle across the entire operating envelope — not just the cells you happened to test.

What You Get
📊 Complete VE Coverage

~98% of all VE table cells populated, including part-throttle and cruise — vs ~80% with traditional methods.
